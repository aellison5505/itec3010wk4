Fear of the Unknown

Many firefighters may be reluctant to see a doctor over a fear of being told they can't do the job anymore or they need to make changes in lifestyle. Little do these firefighters realize they not only put their lives at risk, but they put the lives of their fellow fighters at risk as well. Each firefighter has a job to do and if one piece fails the entire crew is now in jeopardy.

Medical Check-UP

A firefighter should have a yearly physical. The physical should include the following listed items.

  • Heart ECG
  • Lung X-Rayay
  • Vision Test
  • Hearing Test
  • Lab Work-Up
