Proper Diet

As a firefighter a proper diet is must consisting of mostly protein, vegetables, and fruits. Firefighting is a physical job, therefore starches and whole grains are a must for energy. And lets not forget the healthy fats.

Drinking plenty of water is also critical for proper body functions.

Proteins

A firefighter should cinsume plenty of proteins.

  • Eggs
  • Chicken
  • Pork
  • Soy
  • Fish

Red meat should be consumed in limited quantities as it is high in fat and cholesterol.

Vegetables

Vegetables are important sources of many nutrients, including potassium, dietary fiber, folate (folic acid), vitamin A, and vitamin C.

  • Tomatoes
  • Spinach
  • Broccoli
  • Cabbage
  • Asparagus

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are the body's main source of fuel and are easily used by the body for energy.

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Yams
  • Whole Grains
  • Pasta

Healthy Fats

Consuming healthy fats can lead to more energy, better health, and reduce inflammation.

  • Walnuts
  • Almonds
  • Pecans
  • Peanuts
