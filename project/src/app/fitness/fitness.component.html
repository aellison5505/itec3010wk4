Fit to Fight

A firefighter needs to be cardiovascularly fit by doing a 60-minute workout 3 to 4 times a week. Everyone always thinks cardio and running go hand and hand. Well, most firefighters will not run for any reason. Here are a few non-running cardio workouts.

Circuit Training

  • Do exercises with minimal rest about 15 seconds between each set.
  • Keep your heart rate up.
  • Pork
  • Do the exercises you enjoy.
  • Large muscle group lifts or concentrated moves work best.

Cardio Machines

Indoor machines are great exercises that are low impact on the body but great for your heart.

  • Stair Mill
  • Rowing Machine
  • Airdyne
  • Spin Bike
  • Elliptical

High-Intensity Interval Training

High-intensity interval training (HIIT) is any workout that alternates between intense bursts of activity. Just remember to rest up using a 2:1 ratio.

  • Sprints
  • Lunges
  • Speed Bags
